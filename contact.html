  FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the initial consultation really free?
Yes, your first 30-minute consultation is completely free with no obligation. We believe everyone deserves access to legal guidance before committing to representation.
How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?
We respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please call our emergency line (+91 98765 43210) for immediate assistance.
Is my information kept confidential?
Absolutely. All communications with LexCounsel are protected by strict attorney-client privilege. Your information is never shared with any third party.
What documents should I bring to the consultation?
Bring any relevant documents related to your matter — court notices, contracts, property papers, correspondence etc. Even if you have nothing, we can guide you from scratch.
Do you offer online consultations?
Yes, we offer video consultations via Zoom, Google Meet or WhatsApp Video for clients who cannot visit in person. Simply mention your preference when booking.
