  Who We Are

More Than a Law Firm —
We Are Your Legal Partners

Founded in 2004 by Senior Advocate Arjun Mehta, LexCounsel began as a boutique litigation practice in the heart of New Delhi. Over two decades, we have grown into one of India's most respected law firms — not by compromising on quality, but by delivering exceptional results one client at a time.

Our philosophy is simple: every client deserves the same quality of representation as the most powerful institution. We combine the resources and expertise of a large firm with the personalised attention and commitment of a boutique practice.

  • 12 specialised advocates across 6 practice areas
  • Registered with Bar Council of India & Delhi High Court
  • Empanelled counsel for multiple government bodies
  • 3500+ cases successfully handled since 2004
  • 98% client satisfaction rate consistently maintained
  • Free 30-minute consultation for every new client
